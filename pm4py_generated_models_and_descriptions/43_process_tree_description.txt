Use Case: Recruitment Process
This process model starts with Prepare Proposal, drafting a comprehensive plan, and then Hire Candidate, finalizing the selection of personnel. Concurrently, another path proceeds to Conduct Interviews, gathering important information. It then branches into Review Budget, a detailed examination of financial resources. After this step there are two alternatives: Evaluate Options, assessing various possibilities, or a sequence involving Finalize Features, defining the key elements, parallel to a choice between Develop Design, creating a detailed plan, or Generate Requirements, listing essential criteria.
