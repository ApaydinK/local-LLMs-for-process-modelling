### fill shipment is done multiple times ###
Use Case: Food delivery

This process model starts with Prepare Order when order is received. It then moves to Check Inventory, ensuring that the required items are in stock. Following this, it branches into the task to Fill Shipment, where the order is packed and prepared for dispatch. The follow-up process then offers two alternatives: Review Payment, verifying the transaction details, or Confirm Shipping, ensuring that the shipment details are accurate and the package is ready for delivery.
