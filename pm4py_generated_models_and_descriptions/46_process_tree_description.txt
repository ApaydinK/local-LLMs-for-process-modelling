Use Case: Food delivery

This process model starts with Prepare Order when order is received.Then seller Checks Inventory, ensuring that the required items are in stock. Following this, it branches into the task to Fill Shipment, where the order is packed and prepared for dispatch. The follow-up process then need two employees. One will Review Payment, verifying the transaction details, the other will Confirm Shipping, ensuring that the shipment details are accurate and the package is ready for delivery.
