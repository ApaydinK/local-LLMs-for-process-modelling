##plan meeting and the parallel path is a third option to execute the entire process ###
Use case: Recruitment/Panel-discussion

This process model begins and end with Conduct Interview, gathering crucial insights and information. Othwise the model begins and ends with Review Document, which involves thoroughly examining relevant materials. The process continues with Plan Meeting, organizing a session for further discussion. This step provides two additional options: Schedule Follow-up, arranging a subsequent meeting for ongoing dialogue, or Send Report, disseminating findings and decisions.
