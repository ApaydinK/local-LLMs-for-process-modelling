The process begins with preparing an order, which is a crucial step in ensuring that customer needs are met. Once the order is prepared, there are two possible paths forward. After preparing the order, we move on to processing the payment for the order, which is a critical step in completing the transaction. Finally, we fulfill the order by delivering the goods or services to the customer. Parallel, to the steps after prepare order, our team will receive a customer query regarding their order and thus handle the customer query.
