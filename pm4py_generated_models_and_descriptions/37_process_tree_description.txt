Use Case: Project meeting

This process begins with Oversee Project Meeting, ensuring that the meeting is effectively planned and coordinated. It then progresses to two parallel tasks: Hold Project Meeting to facilitate discussion and Conduct Team Review to evaluate team performance. Following these activities, it transitions to Review Reports, focusing on analyzing and assessing report details. This step involves a detailed review of Report Content, ensuring accuracy and completeness, followed by two alternatives: Review Report Format to ensure proper formatting or Submit Report, finalizing the process.
